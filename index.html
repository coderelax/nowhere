Policy and Invoice Management

Automated renewals, cancellation processes, policy statistics, custom policy/invoice templates, collecting payments and more.

Policy Bordereaux Reports

Automate the creation of bordereaux reports, with support for multiple templates, including Lloyd's. Highly customizable.

CRM and Customer Portals

Organize and stay on top of custom relations. Empower customers with self-service tools.

Dynamic Rating Guides

Goodbye to error-prone spreadsheets. Make rating guide updates easy and have a system that automatically gives you the correct rates.

Online application forms

Allow customers to securely apply online to new insurances. Integrate forms with external services. Reduce manual data entry by having customers fill out forms.

Sales commission calculations

Automatically calculate commissions for salespeople, sub-brokers and others where each party may have a very different formula.

Claim management and handling

Manage processes and invoices related to claims. Allow customers to submit claim reports online.

Electronic signatures

We help digitize all aspects of insurance work with electronic signatures being a key part of it, reducing the amount of paperwork and manual data entry.

Data-driven decision making

Having policies and other key information well organized allows for a deeper analysis that can prove exceptionally helpful and revealing in decision making.

Automated workflows

Renewals, bordereaux reporting, new customer application processes, payment collections, customer follow-ups  and more can  be automated.
